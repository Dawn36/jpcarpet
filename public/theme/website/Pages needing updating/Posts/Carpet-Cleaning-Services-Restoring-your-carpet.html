Carpet Cleaning Services in Beverly Hills: Restoring It’s Original Look

27 feb

There are many people who perform regular vacuuming and take pride in maintaining a spic and span house. But the reality is different. You believe or not, your carpets are actually dirtier than you expect. The humans naked eye cannot see the dirt, allergens, and bacteria trapped within the fibers of your carpet. If you miss the annual or bi-annual professional carpet cleaning services, your carpet could become the breeding ground for bacteria and dust mites.

Dust mites are the living organism that eats the dead skin cells we shed; hence they can increase in the home environment easily.

Here are some issues that your carpets can cause.

Respiration Issues

You can miss out on the molds in your carpets. Usually, everyone looks out for mold in walls and wooden structures. If you have colorful carpets in your home, the designs can conceal the spots that have mold on it.

The molds are active in humid months and attract bacteria and allergens. Continuous exposure and inhaling such allergens can cause flu-like symptoms and respiration issues. Moreover, avoiding professional carpet cleaning services can also trigger serious health issues such as asthma attacks.

Skin Issues

Except than dust mites, a dirty carpet can hide carpet beetles and fleas in the fibers. The eggs can survive in the fibers for many years. Once they finally hatch, they can create lots of problems for your pets. It’s a cyclical problem since animal fur is one of the things such insects thrive on. As for the humans of the house, the bodies of the dust mites can cause rashes and eye irritation.

Your Immune System is Endangered

Your body fights with toxins every second of every day to keep you in good health. Living in a clean room but dirty carpets can cause excessive stress on the fighting ability of your immune system. Ultimately, with the bacteria build-up, your immune system will begin to give in. that’s how you can begin contracting illnesses.

The most dangerous type of bacteria are Mycotoxins and this can be found in your carpets if professional carpet cleaning services are avoided. It can weaken your immunity, making you susceptive to various types of illnesses, most common of which are respiratory tract and stomach infections.

Deep Carpet Cleaning Services Can Promote Healthy Lifestyle

Regular vacuuming of carpet will help to keep it clean on the outer surface but when it comes to deep cleaning you need to call professionals for carpet cleaning services at least once a year.
