So what is Strip and Wax?
Strip and Wax is exactly what the name suggests. Some flooring, especially those for hospitals and doctors offices, is waxed, and several months after the layer of wax is applied, it begins to wear down. This is normal and expected considering the amount of foot traffic where this flooring type is used. But just because it is to be expected, doesn't not mean it needs to be accepted. Once the wax layer starts wearing out, that's where we come in. We remove the previous layer of wax, along with all the dirt, dust, and scuffs collected on that wax, steam clean the floor underneath to ensure a completely clean floor, then we carefully apply the wax.

Why should I put things on a schedule?
Life can be stressful. With kids, and pets, and work, and on and on and on, who has time to think about cleaning? You already spend so much time trying to keep your countertops tidy, how could anyone ask you to maintain your carpet or tile? By having the cleaning on a schedule, we take some of that stress for you, so you don't need to worry about it. You simply pick times throughout the year that work for you and we take care of the rest.
