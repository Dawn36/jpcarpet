Tile and Grout Cleaning
If you’re looking for professional tile grout cleaning services in Los Angeles, CA then you came to the right place! We offer experienced tile and grout cleaners, who will be able to help you return your household tiles to a beautiful, pristine condition, no matter whether they’re ceramic, stone, marble or slate! Likewise, we have earned a great reputation for our tile and grout cleaning and stone restoration in Los Angeles and the San Fernando Valley, and we believe it comes as a direct result of the high standards we hold.

Here’s a quick overview of our tile cleaning process:

- Pre-treat the tile and grout with our 100% Eco-Friendly products to loosen any embedded dirt or soil
- Scrub your tiles around every fixture and crevice, with our state-of-the-art tools and equipment
- Steam clean your tile and grout
- Walk-through with you to ensure satisfaction

We provide our tile and grout cleaning services for both your home and business, including tiled counters, walls, and of course, floors.

Our Tile and Grout Cleaning Services include:
- Complete Ceramic Tile and Grout Cleaning
- Hand Scrubbing
- Scrub Grout to Remove Stains
- Ceramic Tile Polishing
- Sealing Grout For Stain Protection

Regular maintenance of your tiled floors and bathrooms, such as moping or wiping down with a neutral cleaner is a very important part of keeping your tile and grout pristine. Combined with a yearly professional steam cleaning of your tile and grout, you will keep your tiles like new for years to come.
